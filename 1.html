About RV University Sports Mania

Welcome to the RVU Sports Mania, where we celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship and healthy competition. It is an opportunity for all RVU students to showcase their athletic abilities and compete in various sports events. The event is designed to promote physical fitness, team spirit, and a healthy lifestyle among students. Join us for an action-packed week of sports and excitement.
